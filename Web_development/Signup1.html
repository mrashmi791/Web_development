Welcome To Shanti Niketan School

WHY SHANTI NIKETAN PUBLIC SCHOOL ?

Shanti Niketan Public School is a co-educational English medium school situated in the New Bypass, Jaganpura,Near Brahmpur Pul Patna . The school imparts education from Play Group to the std 10th. It is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi, and is managed by a SchoolManaging Committee The School aims to encourage the all round development of a child, fostering in him / her a love for excellence tempered by compassion and human values. The current enrolment of students stands at an impressive 2000(approx).

The institution came into being with the basic objective of providing quality education to the wards of Shanti Niketan employees. Shanti Niketan Public School institutions have rendered a unique service, in not only dispelling ignorance and illiteracy but also in producing patriots, freedom-fighters, social reformers, intellectuals, thinkers, legal luminaries, doctors, engineers and scientists of unparalleled merit, integrity and caliber.

Now it has taken up a larger role and provides education to various segments of society and aims to inculcate moral values, social responsibility and provides environment required for all round development of personality with all necessary traits
